Leave Application Overview

Leave Policy Summary

Employees are entitled to annual, sick, maternity, paternity, emergency, and unpaid leave in accordance with Malaysian labor laws, with specific eligibility and application requirements for each type.

Instructions and Rules for Leave Application

1. Submit a leave application form or online request at least 7 days in advance.

2. Submit a medical certificate for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days.

3. Submit a maternity leave form along with a medical certificate confirming pregnancy and expected confinement date.

4. No application required; leave is automatically granted on designated public holidays.

5. Submit a request for compassionate leave with relevant documentation (e.g., death certificate, hospital admission note).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Click the "Apply for Leave" button below and fill out the form.

Q: What happens after I submit my leave application?
A: Kindly wait for your manager to review and approve or reject your application.
